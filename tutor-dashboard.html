💳 Payout Setup
Students pay through frNtcOda's Paystack. Course enrollment: 93% to you, 7% to frNtcOda. Certificate fees: 50/50 split.
How to get your Subaccount Code
  1. Go to dashboard.paystack.com
  2. Click Settings → Subaccounts
  3. Click Create Subaccount and fill your bank details
  4. Copy the code — it looks like ACCT_xxxxxxxxxx
  5. Paste it below and save
This is how you receive your 93% share of student payments.
