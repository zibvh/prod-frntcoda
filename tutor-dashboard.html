How to get your Paystack Subaccount Code
  1. Go to dashboard.paystack.com
  2. Click Settings → Subaccounts
  3. Click Create Subaccount and fill your bank details
  4. Copy the code — it looks like ACCT_xxxxxxxxxx
  5. Paste it below and save
How to get your Flutterwave Subaccount ID
  1. Go to dashboard.flutterwave.com
  2. Click Settings → Sub Accounts
  3. Click Create Sub Account and fill your bank details
  4. Copy the ID — it looks like FSA_xxxxxxxxxx
  5. Paste it below and save
